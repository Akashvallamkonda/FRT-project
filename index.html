Benefits of Trees

Trees play a crucial role in maintaining a healthy environment. They provide a wide range of benefits, such as oxygen production, air purification, and more.

Conservation of Biodiversity

Trees are home to various species of birds, insects, and mammals. They help in preserving biodiversity and maintaining the ecological balance of our planet.

Carbon Sequestration

Trees absorb carbon dioxide during photosynthesis, helping to mitigate climate change by reducing greenhouse gas levels in the atmosphere.

Shade and Cooling

Trees provide shade and help cool the environment, making urban areas more comfortable and energy-efficient, reducing the need for air conditioning.
